About Black Lives Matter

Black Lives Matter is a project originally started as a small data visualization assignment. I recognized its potential early on to be used as a valuable tool. The objective is to visually showcase the overwhelming mass amount of Police shootings that has taken a toll on this country over the past couple of years. Police shootings are mundane; people are becoming numb to everyday police brutality, especially since the media doesn't tend to focus on the most important aspects of the case. Many in the general public believe the shootings are racially motivated: various organizations have been started based with the aim to abolish police shootings and shed light on what is happening in our communities yet without solid proof of this racial connection, their cries sadly fall on deaf ears. The general population can easily deny the racial bias within the policing system simply because there is not enough available evidence to stimulate change. This site hopes to provide that visual evidence.
